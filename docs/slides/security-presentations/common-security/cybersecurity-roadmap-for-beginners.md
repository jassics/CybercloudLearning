## Key Takeaways

- The field is short roughly 3.4 million professionals globally (ISC2 2022 Cybersecurity Workforce Study) - the demand side of this roadmap is real, not hype.
- "Never give up" attitude and attention to detail matter more early on than any specific tool - the hacker mindset is about deep-diving and staying adaptive, not memorizing commands.
- Linux fundamentals (file structure, `grep`/`find`/`sed`/`awk`, `whois`/`curl`/`wget`/`openssl`) are foundational regardless of which specialization you pick.
- Soft skills - clear written communication, public speaking, and "fill with knowledge not ego" - are called out explicitly as differentiators, not afterthoughts.
