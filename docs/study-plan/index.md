## How to Use These Study Plans

1. **Start with Common Skills** - Build foundational knowledge
2. **Choose your path** - Pick a role-specific study plan
3. **Study 3-4 hours daily** - Consistency is key
4. **Hands-on practice** - Labs and real-world scenarios
5. **Track your progress** - Check off completed topics
